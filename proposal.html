Jiayi's Food Site

All about these 4 amazing dishes.


  • What is your website about?

    My website will be about 4 of the most classic Chinese dishes(my favorite), with information on its history, ingredients and tutorials on how to make it.

  • What is the objective of your website? (Also read as why would this site be important?)

    The goal to reveal the history and cultural background behind the Chinese cuisine. While there are countless tutorials on every possible dish in every cuisine, few know why the food came into place. Especially in China, where food is an essential part of the its culture, there should similarly be a deeper understanding on what we are putting in to our mouth.

  • Who is your target audience set?

    The target audience is anyone who is interested in the Chinese dishes. Whether they intend to make them, learn about them, the website aim to educate its audience without them even realizing.

  • What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?

    • Page 1: Mapo Tofu (Sichuan cuisine)
    • Page 2: Dim Sum (Cantonese cuisine)
    • Page 3: Xiao Longbao (Shanghai/Southern cuisine)
    • Page 4: Peking Duck (Beijing cuisine)
